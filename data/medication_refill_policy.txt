MEDICATION REFILL POLICY (Synthetic Example)
Version: 2026-06-02
Clinic Name (Synthetic): Harborview Family & Urgent Care (HFUC)

IMPORTANT NOTE
- Synthetic content for RAG testing only.
- Medication decisions require clinician judgment and patient-specific assessment.

1) STANDARD REFILL TIMELINES
- Routine refills: allow 2 business days.
- Complex requests (dose changes, missing labs): 3–5 business days.
- Weekend/holiday requests: processed next business day.

2) HOW TO REQUEST A REFILL
Preferred methods:
- Patient portal refill request
- Pharmacy electronic refill request
Alternative:
- Phone message with medication name, dose, and preferred pharmacy

3) REQUIRED INFORMATION
- Medication name and strength
- Current dose and frequency
- Preferred pharmacy (name + location)
- Any new symptoms or side effects

4) REFILL LIMITATIONS
4.1 Controlled substances (synthetic policy)
- HFUC generally does not refill controlled substances via portal messages.
- May require an appointment (often in-person) and identity verification.
- Early refill requests may be denied.

4.2 High-risk medications
- Some medications require recent labs or monitoring (e.g., kidney function tests).
- Refills may be limited until monitoring is up to date.

5) APPOINTMENT REQUIREMENTS
- New medication requests typically require a visit.
- Chronic meds may require periodic follow-up (e.g., every 6–12 months) to continue refills.

6) EMERGENCY SUPPLY
- HFUC may provide a limited emergency supply at clinician discretion.
- Not guaranteed; not available for all medications.

7) LOST OR STOLEN MEDICATION
- Report to pharmacy and, if required, local authorities.
- Replacement requests are evaluated case-by-case.

8) PRESCRIPTION TRANSFERS
- Contact your pharmacy to transfer prescriptions between locations when allowed.

9) EXAMPLES FOR RAG TESTING
Q: “How long do routine refills take?”
A: Allow 2 business days.

Q: “Can I request controlled substance refills through the portal?”
A: Generally no; appointment/verification may be required.

Q: “What info do I need for a refill request?”
A: Name/strength, dose, pharmacy, side effects.

END OF DOCUMENT
