APPOINTMENT POLICY (Synthetic Example)
Version: 2026-06-02
Clinic Name (Synthetic): Harborview Family & Urgent Care (HFUC)

IMPORTANT NOTE
- This document is synthetic and for software/RAG testing only.

1) APPOINTMENT TYPES
- New patient visit: 30–40 minutes
- Established patient visit: 15–20 minutes
- Annual wellness exam: 40 minutes
- Urgent same-day visit: 10–15 minutes
- Telehealth video/phone: varies (see Telehealth Policy)

2) HOW TO SCHEDULE
- Online portal (preferred) or phone.
- Patients may be asked to select a reason for visit to match the right appointment length.

3) CHECK-IN REQUIREMENTS
- Arrive 10 minutes early for in-person appointments.
- Bring a government ID and insurance card.
- Update: medication list, allergies, pharmacy, emergency contact.

4) LATE ARRIVAL POLICY
- Grace period: 10 minutes.
- If > 10 minutes late, the visit may be rescheduled depending on schedule capacity.
- If the clinician can still see the patient, the visit may be shortened.

5) CANCELLATIONS AND NO-SHOWS
- Cancel/reschedule at least 24 hours in advance.
- No-show definition: missing the appointment without notice, or arriving > 15 minutes late.
- No-show fee (synthetic): $25.
- After 3 no-shows within 12 months, online scheduling may be disabled and future appointments may require a deposit.

6) SAME-DAY AND URGENT VISITS
- Same-day visits are limited and triaged by symptom severity.
- Severe symptoms may be directed to emergency services.

7) WAIT TIMES AND VISIT FLOW
- Patients may experience delays due to urgent clinical needs.
- HFUC will provide updates if delays exceed 20 minutes.

8) ACCOMPANYING VISITORS
- Adults: 1 support person allowed unless space restrictions apply.
- Pediatrics: parent/guardian required.
- Infection control: visitors may be asked to wear masks during respiratory illness season.

9) FORMS, REFERRALS, AND PRIOR AUTHORIZATIONS
- Routine forms: allow 3–5 business days.
- Disability/FMLA forms: may require an appointment.
- Prior authorizations: timelines depend on payer; allow 7–14 days.

10) PAYMENT POLICY (Synthetic)
- Copays due at check-in.
- Self-pay rates:
  - Office visit (established): $120
  - New patient: $170
  - Annual wellness: $210
- Payment methods: credit/debit, HSA/FSA.

11) EXAMPLES FOR RAG TESTING
Q: “What happens if I’m 12 minutes late?”
A: Visit may be rescheduled; if seen, may be shortened.

Q: “How far in advance must I cancel to avoid the fee?”
A: At least 24 hours.

Q: “Can I bring a family member with me?”
A: Usually one support person.

END OF DOCUMENT
